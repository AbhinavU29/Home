# PRD: Housewarming E-Card Website - "ApAA"

## 1. Project Overview
The goal is to create a minimalist, high-end digital invitation for the housewarming ceremony of **ApAA**. The website will serve as a single-page invitation for guests, providing essential event details and navigation.

---

## 2. Core Content & Sections

### Section 1: Hero / Welcome
* **Headline:** All are welcome to my home!
* **House Name:** ApAA
* **Visual:** A high-resolution, welcoming photo of the house exterior or the nameplate.

### Section 2: Ceremony Details
* **Primary Event:** Housewarming Ceremony
* **Time:** 09:00 AM
* **Date:** [Insert Date Here]
* **Aesthetic:** Bold, elegant typography to emphasize the single event time.

### Section 3: Gallery (Home Photos)
* **Content:** A curated selection of 3–5 photos of the new home.
* **Format:** A clean image grid or a smooth horizontal slider.
* **Purpose:** To give guests a "sneak peek" of the new space.

### Section 4: Location & Navigation
* **Address Text:** [Insert Full Address Here]
* **Interactive Map:** Embedded Google Maps widget for real-time navigation.
* **Action Button:** "Open in Google Maps" button for mobile users.

---

## 3. Visual & Design Requirements
* **Theme:** Modern Minimalist.
* **Color Palette:** Soft neutrals (Cream/Beige) with a sophisticated accent color (e.g., Deep Gold or Forest Green).
* **Typography:** A mix of a classic Serif font for headings and a clean Sans-Serif for details.
* **Imagery:** Professional-style photography of **ApAA** with warm, natural lighting.

---

## 4. Functional Requirements
* **Mobile Responsive:** The site must look perfect on smartphones, as most guests will open it via WhatsApp or SMS.
* **One-Tap Navigation:** Clicking the map must trigger the user's GPS app.
* **Fast Loading:** Optimized images to ensure the invite opens instantly even on mobile data.
